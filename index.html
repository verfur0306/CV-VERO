JOURNALIST & JR DEVELOPER

I am a journalist, writer and TV producer with +8 years of experience. Since August 2021 I've been studying Web Development, I continue to do so. I feel ready to work as a developer, copywriter o UX writer, but I haven't had the chance yet. I would like to get my first job in the IT sector to finally cristalize my jump from journalism to this new world.

I learn fast and I am really motivated. I feel comfortable in challenging situations and ever-changing environments (I used to work on a 24-hr News TV Station!)

Storytelling is my passion. Writing can be quite lonely so I also enjoy tasks that involve taking part of a group. I like brainstorming and I get motivated by team objectives.

I worked in Todo Noticias (TN), one of the largest TV networks in Latin America. I was in charge of the night shift, from 8 pm to 1 am. I managed a group of 6 people in order to make the night edition, a program called TN de Noche.

With regards to TV Producing, I've been everywhere: from picking up the phone to planning new products or special transmissions. I consider the videograph a key asset and I am proud to say that I have worked many years on it.

One of the most difficult lessons that I've learnt these past years has been to work with multidisciplinary teams. After hitting my head against the wall several times, I finally understood that not everyone has the same level of motivation or sense of urgence and that people are more important than their hierarchy. I am more mature, I developed interpersonal skills and now I enjoy a good debate or a negotiation to get my ideas through.

I am very keen on writing. I, of course, write better in Spanish. I've worked for 20 Minutos Spain and Radio France International. Currently I collaborate as a correspondent for El Confidencial (Spain).

A bit about me:

I was born in Junin de los Andes, Patagonia Argentina. I moved to Buenos Aires when I entered university, just after my 18th birthday.

In 2012-2013 I lived in Barcelona to study a Master's degree in International Journalism. Apart from learning about International Relations and Diplomacy, I gained a beautiful group of Catalan, Spanish and Portuguese friends. That experience led me back to Buenos Aires, to a trainee in Todo Noticias where I currently work.

Last year I started studying Javascript and I discovered that I like web desing a lot. I made this webpage from scratch using CSS and HTML. I am moving back to Barcelona with my family on September 6th. I have Italian citizenship and Spanish NIE.
