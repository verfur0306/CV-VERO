VERÓNICA FURLAN


JOURNALIST & JR DEVELOPER

Behind every computer or mobile device there is a person. Behind every post, a thought.
My objective in life is to make the internet a little less angry and a little kinder.
I am journalist willing to explore the digital and corporate worlds. I have experience in TV Broadcasting and news writing for Argentina and Spain.
Storytelling is my passion, I like working within a group and I get motivated by big objectives.
I command a ship called "TN de Noche" and I am also part of a team called "Solo una Vuelta Más". This two programs are transmitted by TN, one of the largest News chains in Latin America.
With regards to TV Producing, I've been everywhere: from picking up the phone to planning new products or organizing specials. I consider the videograph a key asset and I proudly work on them.
One of the most difficult lessons that I've learnt these past years has been to work with multidisciplinary teams. I finally got to see that not everyone has the same motivation and that people are more important than the place they occupy within the company. I am more mature, I developed interpersonal skills and now I enjoy a good debate or a negotiation to get my ideas through.
I am very keen on writing. I, of course, write way better in Spanish. I've written for 20 Minutos España and Radio France International. Currently I collaborate as a correspondent for El Confidencial (Spain).
A bit about me:
I was born in Junin de los Andes, Patagonia Argentina. I moved to Buenos Aires at the age of 18.
In 2012-2013 I lived in Barcelona to study my Master's degree. Apart from learning about International Relations and Diplomacy, I gained a beautiful group of Catalan, Spanish and Portuguese friends. That experience led me back to Buenos Aires, to a trainee in Todo Noticias where I currently work.
Last year I started studying Javascript and I discovered that I like web desing a lot. I made this webpage from scratch using CSS and HTML. I am living in Buenos Aires but I am willing to live in Italy (I have citizenship).
